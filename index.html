Upshur Engine Works
Model Farm Engine Plans designed by Hamilton Upshur

Upshur Engine Works was founded by Hamilton Upshur in 1944 with a desire to spread the joy of model engine building to the world.

Educational Value

Hamilton's designs have often been used by schools and universities and have been admired for their ease of use, quality instruction and simple designs. Beginners are recommended to start with the Red Farm Engine Plans

Giving Back

In the past Hamilton would use the proceeds from the sale of his plans to further his involvement in the hobby; since his passing all profits from the sale of his plans have been donated to a deserving charity. Follow our Blog to see who we've donated to in the past or to submit your charity for future donation consideration.

Digital or Analog

While we understand the value of having a paper copy of your plans while working on a model engine, we have made available all of Hamilton's engine plans in PDF form making them less susceptible to wear and tear and allowing for swifter delivery to the hungry model engine building masses.

Engine Plans

Each set of plans contain drawings covering construction of:
-Spark plug,
-Fuel tank,
-Piston rings,
-Valve seating tool,
-Ignition diagram,
-Optional spoked flywheels.
All are engines are designed to be fabricated of aluminum, brass and steel stock without castings. All engines run on gasoline with spark ignition. A single set of plans costs $10.00 plus $3.00 postage. Plans are currently only available on plain copy paper. Digital (PDF) copies available soon!
